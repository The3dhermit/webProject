DICKUS BUTTICUS

funny sence July 2nd, 2006

About

Dick Butt is an illustration of an anthropomorphic phallus with a pair of testicles and a penis protruding from its backside. In multi-pane image macros and animated GIFs, the drawing is often revealed unexpectedly in the final frame.

Origin

On July 2nd, 2006, artist K.C. Green published a webcomic in his titled “Tree. You’ve Been Good to Us" as part of the Horribleville series, which featured an artist who illustrates the Dick Butt character after being scolded for wasting tree resources as a child (shown below).

Spread

On July 16th, 2010, DeviantArtist beyx uploaded an illustration of a Dick Butt character with large eyes sitting on a rainbow (shown below). On October 30th, 2011, the Xkcdickbutt Tumblr blog was launched, highlighting Dick Butt comics illustrated in the style of the Xkcd series (shown below, right).

On April 20th, 2012, FunnyJunk user atlantaboy submitted a screenshot of Dick Butt used in a 4chan thread (shown below). On December 11th, 2013, the Internet humor site College Humor posted a compilation of notable Dick Butt animated GIFs.
